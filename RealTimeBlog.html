How I built RealTime

August 5, 2025

Long story short, I built RealTime to let friends share temporary stories…

Why temporary stories?

It seemed everyone was craving more genuine, in-the-moment sharing—so I built RealTime to expire posts after 30 minutes, keeping the feed fresh and authentic.
